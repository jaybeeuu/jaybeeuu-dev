SOLID
Excellent programming is about more than just syntax and reserved words. SOLID principles give me a firm basis for developing maintainable and effective code.

SOLID

A big part of my mission as a programmer has always been not just to "be able to do it", but to "be able to do it in the right way". A quality programmer is not just the one with the most keywords memorised or an uncanny ability to use obscure syntax, but one able to produce readable, maintainable, extendible & durable code quickly and efficiently.

In order to learn the "right way" I have spent a long time studying the SOLID priciples which are an industry recognised method of doing just that. During this quest I have taken courses, read books talked to colleagues and friends and generally made it my business to understand as much as possible about the SOLID methodoloy.
